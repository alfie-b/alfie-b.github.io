The Linfall Craftsman

After thirty years leading schools, former headmaster Colin Moore has stepped out of the classroom to pursue his passion for sculpting, painting and working with wood.

Together with his musician wife, Josie, he has set up home in the rolling landscape of the Scottish Borders; gloriously remote but close enough to enjoy the rich culture of Scotland’s capital city. His studio/workshop is a converted stable block three miles outside of Peebles.

Son of artist John Moore (Guys), Colin is one of seven siblings all of whom are practising amateur or professional artists.
